OUR STORY

California Roleplay was founded in 2022 by a group of dedicated Emergency Response: Liberty County enthusiasts who wanted to create a more immersive and structured roleplay experience.

What started as a small community has grown into one of the most respected roleplay servers, known for its attention to detail, friendly community, and commitment to quality roleplay.

Our staff team works tirelessly to ensure our server remains a welcoming environment for both new and experienced roleplayers alike, with regular events, training sessions, and community activities.

We pride ourselves on our realistic approach to emergency services roleplay while maintaining a fun and engaging atmosphere for all players.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Find answers to common questions about California Roleplay.

FAQ

How do I join California Roleplay? +

You can join our Roblox game by searching for "Emergency Response: Liberty County" and looking for our server named "California Roleplay". Make sure to also join our Discord server to stay updated!

Do I need experience to join? +

No experience is necessary! We welcome all players regardless of experience level. We offer training programs for those who want to join emergency services departments.

What are the server rules? +

Our complete rules list is available in our Discord server. The main rules include no exploiting, being respectful to all players, and maintaining realistic roleplay at all times.

How do I join a department? +

After playing as a civilian for a while, you can apply to join a department through our Discord server. Each department has its own application process and requirements.

Is there an age requirement? +

While there's no strict age requirement, we expect all players to maintain mature roleplay. Most of our community members are 13+.

Can I suggest new features? +

Absolutely! We welcome community feedback and suggestions. You can submit ideas through our Discord server's suggestions channel.
